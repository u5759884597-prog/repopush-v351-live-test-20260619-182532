# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Participants in this project are expected to be respectful, constructive, and inclusive.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Provide actionable feedback.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, doxxing, or sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns through the support channel listed in SUPPORT.md. Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when needed.
